Biography

My journey began with a deep curiosity about how numbers tell stories — long before I knew the term "data analyst," I was already fascinated by patterns hidden in everyday information.

Growing up in Egypt, I pursued Computer and Information Sciences at Zagazig University, where I discovered the intersection of logic, creativity, and technology that would define my career.

During my studies, I fell in love with programming and data visualization, realizing that the most powerful solutions emerge when analytical rigor meets elegant design.

After graduation, I immersed myself in real-world projects — from building predictive models to crafting full-stack web applications — each experience sharpening both my technical toolkit and my problem-solving mindset.

Today, I operate at the crossroads of data science and software development, helping organizations make informed decisions while delivering seamless digital experiences.

Every dataset I touch and every application I build reflects a single belief: technology should empower people, simplify complexity, and create lasting value.
